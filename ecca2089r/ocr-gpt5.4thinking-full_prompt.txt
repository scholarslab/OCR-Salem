Mary Warrin affirmed to ye Jury of Inquest That she hath
been ~~grievously~~ afflicted by Giles Cory or his apperrition and
that by beating of me with his staffe: & by biting mee &
pinching & choaking me, greatly tormenting me & cutting me
with a knife. & particularly at ye time of his examination
he did grievously torment me: also: at the time of his
examination. I saw: sd Cory or his apparition most dread-
fully afflict: Mary Walcot Ann putnam: mercy lewis &
Sarah: Vibber    Septr. 9: 1692

Eli: Woodwell upon ye oath she formerly has taken in
this court, did affirm to ye Jury of Inquest That she saw
Giles Cory at meeting at Salem on a lecture day, since
he has bin at prison he or his apparition: came in &
sat in ye middlemost seat: of ye mens seats: by ye post
his was stature day before. Bridget Bishop was hanged
and I saw him come out with ye rest of ye people. ~~it~~ ~~this~~
mary Walcot affirmed: if saw sd Cory: as above, sit in ye
same place at ye same time he or his appearance & it she
did se him go out with ye rest of ye people: this she affirmd
to ye Jury of Inquest Sept 9: 1692

Eli: Hubbard to ye Jury of Inquest that Giles Cory hath several
times afflicted me with several sorts of torments; I verily think
he is a wizard & afflicted me by witchcraft    sept 9: 1692
