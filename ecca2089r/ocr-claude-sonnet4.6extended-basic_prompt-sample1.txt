Mary Warren affirmed: to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that she hath been: [struck: she] afflicted by Giles Cory or his apparrition and that by beating of me with his Staffe: & by biting me pinching & choaking me greatly tormenting me. & cutting me with a knife. & perticularly at yᵉ time of his examination he did grievously torment me: also: at the time of his examination: I saw: Sd Cory or his apparition most: dreadfully afflict: Mary Walcot Ann Putnam: Mercy Lewis & Sarah: Vibber Sept². 9. 1692

Eliz Woodwell upon yᵉ oath she formerly has taken in this Court: did affirm to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that she saw Giles Cory at meeting at Salem on a lecture day. Since he has bin at prison he or his apparition: came in & Sat: in yᵉ middlemost Seat: of cl mens Seats: by yᵉ post this was lecture day before: Bridget Bishop was hanged and I saw him come out with yᵉ rest of yᵉ people: at yᵉ time Mary Walcot: affirmed: if Saw Sd Cory: as above: Sitting yᵉ same place at yᵉ same time he or his appearance & if she [struck: she] Se him go out: with yᵉ rest of yᵉ people. this she affirmd to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: Sept² 9: 1692
