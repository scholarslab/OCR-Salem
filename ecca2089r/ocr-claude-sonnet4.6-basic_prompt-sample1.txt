Mary Warrin affirmd: to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that she hath been [stre] afflicted by Giles Cory or his apparrition and that by beating of me with his Staffe: & by biting me pinching & choaking me greatly tormenting me: & cutting me with a knife: & perticulerly at yᵉ time of his examination he did grievously torment me: also: at the time of his examination I saw: Sd Cory or his apparition most: dreadfully afflict: Mary Walcot Ann Putnam: Mercy Lewis & Sarah: Vibber Sept². 9. 1692

Eliz Woodwell upon yᵉ oath she formerly has taken in this court: did affirm to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that she saw Giles Cory at meeting at Salem on a lecture day: since he has bin at prison he or his apparition: came in & Sat: in yᵉ middlemost seat: of cl mens seats: by yᵉ post this was lecture day before: Bridget Bishop was hanged and I saw him come out with yᵉ rest of yᵉ people: at yᵉ time Mary Walcot: affirmed: if Sd Cory: as above: sitting yᵉ same place at yᵉ same time he or his appearance & if she [stre] se him go out: with yᵉ rest of yᵉ people: this she affirmd to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: Sept² 9: 1692

Eliz Hubbard: to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that Giles Cory hath Several times afflicted [me] with several sorts of torments: I verily think he is a wizard & afflicted me by witchcraft Sept² 9: 1692
