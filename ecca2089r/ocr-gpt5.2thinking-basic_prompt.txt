Mary Warren affirmed to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that she hath
been oft afflicted by Giles Cory or his apparition and
that by beating of me with his staffe: & by biting me
pincking & choaking me greatly torturing me & cutting me
with a knife. & particularly at yᵉ time of his examination
he did grievously torment me: also: at the time of his
examination I saw: G Cory or his apparition most: did
falsly afflict: Mary Walcot Ann Putnam mary lewis &
Sarah: Wilber    Septʳ 9: 1692

Eliz Woodwell upon yᵉ oath she formerly has taken in
this Court: did affirm to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that she saw
Giles Cory at meeting at Salem on a Lecture Day. since
he has ben at prison he or his apparition came in &
sat in yᵉ middlemost seat of yᵉ mens seats by yᵉ post
this was lecture Day before. Bridget Bishop wᵃˢ hanged
and I saw him come on with yᵉ rest of yᵉ people. at this time
mary Walcot: affirmed: I saw sᵈ Cory; as above. siting in yᵉ
same place at yᵉ same time. he or his appearance & if she
did se him goe out: with yᵉ rest of yᵉ people. this she affirms
to yᵉ Jury of Inquest. septʳ 9: 1692

Eliz Hubbard to yᵉ Jury of Inquest that Giles Cory hath several
times afflicted me with several sort of torments I verily think
he’s a wizard & afflicted me by witchcraft  septʳ 9: 1692
