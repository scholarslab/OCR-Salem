Mary Warrin affirmd: to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that she hath been: ~~Shes~~ afflicted by Giles Cory or his apparrition and that by beating of me with his Staffe: & by biting me pinching & choaking me greatly tormenting me: & cutting me with a knife: & perticulerly at yᵉ time of his examination he did grievously torment me: also: at the time of his examination I saw: Sd Cory or his apparrition most: dred fully afflict: Mary Walcot Ann Putnum: Mercy Lewis & Sarah: Vibber Sept⁰. 9. 1692

Eliz Woodwell upon yᵉ oath she formerly has taken in this court: did affirm to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that she saw Giles Cory at meeting at Salem on a lecture day. Since he has bin at prison he or his apparition: came in & Sat: in yᵉ middlemost Seat: of cl mens Seats: by yᵉ post this was lecture day before: Bridget Bishop was hanged and I saw him come out with yᵉ rest of yᵉ people: ~~at yᵉ time~~ Mary Walcot: affirmed: yᵗ Saw Sd Cory: as above: Sitting yᵉ same place at yᵉ same time he or his appearance & if she ~~Shes~~ Se him gor out: with yᵉ rest of yᵉ people. this she affirmd to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: Septᵉ 9: 1692

Eliz Hubbard: to yᵉ Jury of Inquest that Giles Cory hath Several times afflicted ~~me with~~ Several Sorts of torments: I veryly think he is a wizard & afflicted me by witchcraft Septᵉ 9: 1692
