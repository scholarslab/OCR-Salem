Mary Warrin affirmd: to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that she hath been: ~~[struck through word]~~ afflicted by Giles Cory or his apparrition and that by beating of me with his staffe: & by biting me pinching & choaking me greatly tormenting me: & cutting me with a knife: & perticulerly at yᵉ time of his examination he did grievously torment me: also: at the time of his examination I saw: Sd Cory or his apparrition most: dreadfully afflict: Mary Walcot Ann Putnam: Mercy Lewis & Sarah: Vibber Sept². 9. 1692

Eliz Woodwell upon yᵉ oath she formerly has taken in this Court: did affirm to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that she saw Giles Cory at meeting at Salem on a lecture day: since he has bin at prison he or his apparrition: came in & sat: in yᵉ middlemost seat: of elmens seats: by yᵉ post this was lecture day before: Bridget Bishop was hanged and I saw him come out with yᵉ rest of yᵉ people: at yᵉ time Mary Walcot: affirmed: if said Sd Cory: as above: sitting yᵉ same place at yᵉ same time he or his appearance & if she ~~[word]~~ se him go out: with yᵉ rest of yᵉ people: this she affirmd to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: Sept² 9: 1692

Eliz Hubbard: to yᵉ Jury of Inquest: that Giles Cory hath Several times afflicted me with Several sorts of torments: I verily think he is a wizard & afflicted me by witchcraft Sept² 9: 1692
