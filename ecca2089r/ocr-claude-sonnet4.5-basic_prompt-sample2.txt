Mary Walkin affirmed to y° Jury of Inquest that the said been justly afflicted by Giles Cory or his apparition and that by hurting of me with his shaft & by biting me & pinching & choaking me, greatly tormenting me & cutting me with a knife. & particularly at y° time of his examination he did grievously torment me, also at the time of this examination I saw sd Cory or his apparition most dred fully afflict. Mary Wallat Jn° [John] Stream: Mary lins & Sarah: Vibber Sept'g: 1692

Eliz Woodwell upon y° oath she formerly has taken in this Court: did affirm to y° Jury of Inquest: that: the said Giles Cory at meeting at Salem on a Lecture day. Since he has bin at prison he or his apparition: came in & sat, in y° middlemost seat. of ebeny Seals: by y° post this was before the day before. Byshops Boys was Hanged and I saw him come out with y° rest of y° people. at y° same marry Wallat affirmed, y° this sd Cory, as above, it was y° same time he or his apparance & if she was not wus: with y° rest of y° people. this she affirm to y° Jury of Inquest: Sept'd 9: 1692
